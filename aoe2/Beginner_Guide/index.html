INTRODUCTION

Welcome to my beginner's guide to Age of Empires 2 (AOE2)! I'll try to make this as brief as possible so you can read this ASAP.

The general layout of this guide is that per age, I will list the total number of villagers as well as what each villagers should be working on. Economy is a vital part of this game, and it is the thing that beginners should work on first. Once you have a strong economy, you'll be able to fuel a strong military.

Military compositions will depend on the civilization. I will list what I think is the best military composition for each civ after the advanced tips.

Also, always remember to build houses, otherwise you won't be able to create new villagers/units! The only exception is when you hit the population cap for the game, which is usually 200.

Civilization Choices

I do not recommend the Huns or Chinese for new players as they have a bit of a peculiar start. Any other civilizatiosn are strong in their own way and can be picked!

USEFUL HOTKEYS

Since this is a real time strategy game, hotkeys will be vital to your gameplay as you don't want to have to manually click on everything. However, the sheer number of hotkeys can be overwhelming, so I'll just list the most useful ones here first.

Hotkeys Description
H Jump to your Town Center. Very useful for creating villagers, as you can simply spam H -> AAA (A is the default for creating Town Centers).
Ctrl 1, Ctrl 2, etc. all the way to Ctrl 9 Control Group, or assign units/buildings to a certain number. For example, you can assign your Scout to the key 1 by selecting on your Scout and press Ctrl 1. You can also assign buildings for quick unit creation. For example, I like to assign all my Stables to 6 (Ctrl 6), then once I press 6 I can quickly jump to all my stables and create all the Knights I want.
1, 2, 3, ..., to 9 Quickly jump to your Control groups. If you press 1 once, the camera won't immediately "jump" to your unit/building. If you press 1 twice in quick succession, the camera will immediately jump to where your units/buildings are. Also, for buildings, if you press their Control Group once and then you right click, you will set a Garrison Point. This means that all units created from these buildings will gather at the Garrison Point. This is an easy way to collect all your units together in one place!
, (comma) If there are any idle villagers, this button (, comma by default) will jump to the idle villagers right away. Make them go back to work!

Dark Age Notes:

  • Focus on Food first!
  • Try to find your initial 8 sheep.
  • Lure your 2 boars when you get a bit more used to the game.
  • Place your Lumber Camp/Mining Camp as close to its respective resource as you can so your villagers don't have to walk far.
  • In the same regard, place your Farms (available after you create at least 1 Mill) as close to your Town Center and/or your Mill as possible.
  • Remember to get the Loom technology at the Town Center before you click up to Feudal.

Castle Age Notes:

  • Castle Age is the age where you'll spend most of your time.
  • Build 1-2 more town centers so you can produce more villagers.
  • Research Wheelbarrow, then Hand Cart at your Town Center so your villagers can be more efficient.
  • Make sure to get economy upgrades at your Mill, Lumber Camps, and Mining Camps!
  • You can build your civ's unique units at the Castle.
  • If you are playing an Archer civ, build a University and get Ballistics ASAP
  • Start making military units! Make sure to get upgrades at the Blacksmith for your respective troops. For melee units, get the armor upgrades first. For ranged units, get the range + attack ugprades first.
  • Start making Trade Carts at the Market so you'll have a reliable source of Gold income. Try to go to the furthest market from your market for maximum profit.

Imperial Age Notes:

  • Welcome to the endgame!
  • Make Trade Carts if you're running low on gold.
  • Military units + Siege units (including Trebuchets from your Castle) is your main focus here.
  • Make more villagers if you lose any due to enemy raids.

  • To lure boar, use a villager to attack the boar twice. After that, move the villager back to your town center and garrison him/her (select your luring villager, press G, then left click the town center) and have 4-5 villagers shoot the boar. Remember to ungarrison your luring villager!
    • Video of this being done: Click here.
  • If you want a build order for a Fast Castle (less than 18 min) and then into knights, here it is. The general consensus is that you'll click up to Feudal Age at 28 total population, and Castle Age at 30 total population.
    • Build 2 houses with your initial 3 villagers. Have your Town Center create 4 villagers. 4/15 pop.
    • First 6 villagers on sheep. 7/15 pop.
    • Next 4 on wood. 11/15 pop.
    • 11th villager lure boar. 12/15 pop.
    • 12th villager build a house and a mill next to the berries. 13/20 pop.
    • Next 4 villagers on sheep. Start luring your 2nd boar. 17/20 pop.
    • Next 4 villagers on wood. Build 2 more houses. 21/30 pop.
    • Next 2 villagers go to berries. Make farms as needed. 23/30 pop.
    • Next 3 villagers go to gold. 26/30 pop.
    • Next 2 villagers to go wood. 28/30 pop.
    • Click up to Feudal Age.
    • While you're advancing, build a Barracks. Start walling if needed.

    • In Feudal Age, build a Stable with 2 wood villagers (lumberjacks) and a Blacksmith with 1 wood villager.
    • Create 2 more Villagers. Send them to wood. 30/30 pop.
    • Once your TC (Town Center) finishes creating the 2 villagers, your buildings should be finished. Click up to Castle Age.
    • Get the Lumber Camp upgrade, then the Mill upgrade.
    • Once your berries and sheep run out, make farms.
    • Build houses as needed.
    • Build another Stable. Set both Stables to a control group (e.g. 6)
    • Get the armor upgrades from the Blacksmith.
    • If you have the resources for it, get Bloodlines at the stables.
    • Once you finish advancing to Castle Age, immediately start making knights. This can be done via hotkeys by going to your control group and spamming S. E.g., 6 -> SSSS.
    • Remember to always create villagers at your TC! Build more TCs when you think it is safe to do so.
